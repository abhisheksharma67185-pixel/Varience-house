# Variance House — Real Photography Asset Manifest

The current `assets/life-at-the-house-contact-sheet.jpg` is a pre-cohort editorial placeholder used to validate crops, depth, and responsive layouts. It must not be presented as documentary evidence of a residency that has not happened yet.

All final images should be documentary, lightly graded, and retain natural laboratory or villa colour. Avoid stock-photo staging, artificial 3D objects, exaggerated smoke, neon lighting, or invented scientific results.

## Delivery requirements

- Preferred master format: lossless TIFF or maximum-quality JPEG from the camera.
- Web delivery: AVIF or WebP plus JPEG fallback, sized for its rendered slot.
- Preserve EXIF in masters; remove sensitive location metadata from public exports.
- Obtain explicit participant consent and caption approval.
- Do not publish the villa address, access-control details, or unsafe experimental procedures.
- Provide meaningful alt text describing the visible work, not marketing language.
